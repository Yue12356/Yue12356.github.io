09/10/2019

bite one’s nails

束手无策；咬手指

Tom usually bites his nails once he meets difficulties

in despair

在绝望中

dreadful

adj. 令人畏惧的，可怕的

dreadful words 骇人听闻的话

predominantly

adv. 占主导地位地，显著地

predominance n. 主导地位；优势

broker

n. 经纪人 ~middleman

frenzy

n. 狂乱，疯狂

frenzied adj. 狂暴的，疯狂的

silver lining

(黑暗中的)一线光明

Every cloud has a silver lining

upside

n. 好处，有利的一面

downside n. 缺点，不好的方面

pervasive

adj. 遍布的；弥漫的

a pervasive smell of damp

counterbanlance

n. 平衡抵消物

v. 对…起平衡作用

anything but

决不，根本不

The hotel was anything but cheap.

vulnerable

adj. 易受攻击的，脆弱的

vulnerable groups 脆弱的群体

exploitation

n. 利用；开发

exploit v. 利用；压榨

restraint

n. 抑制；约束

His rage was beyond restraint

reluctantly

adv. 不情愿地；勉强地

reluctance n. 不愿意

hostility

n. 敌意；抵制

hostile adj. 敌意的；不利的

be obliged to sb.

感谢某人
